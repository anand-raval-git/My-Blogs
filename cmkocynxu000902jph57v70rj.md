When establishing infrastructure on the AWS cloud, Identity and Access Management (IAM) is among the first and most critical services to configure. IAM facilitates the creation and management of user accounts, groups, roles, policies, and other access controls. The Nautilus DevOps team is currently in the process of configuring these resources and has outlined the following requirements:

Create an IAM role as below:

1) IAM role name must be `iamrole_ammar`.

![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1767588444883/48bd691f-fb07-492d-a3b5-c315e4f70d89.png align="center")

2) Entity type must be `AWS Service` and use case must be `EC2`.

![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1767588582183/4f31c330-0cb4-4ca0-92ae-c27f533fe95e.png align="center")

3) Attach a policy named `iampolicy_ammar`.
