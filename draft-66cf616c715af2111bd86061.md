### Digital public infrastructure

dpi is backbone of india , dpi referes to foundational digital system and services that built and maintain by goverment jab infrastructure ki baat karte hai to road , railway ki yaad aati hai

infrastructure has 2 part

1. physical infrastructure :- esa infrastructure jisse use bhi kar sakte hai touch bhi karke feel bhi kar sakte hai like road or uska physical presence bhi samne hota hai
    
2. digital infrastructure :- jis infrastructure ka use kar sakte hai lekin touch karke feel nahi kar sakte hai , ex :- upl or inka physical presence nahi hota hai
    

digital infrastructure :- dpi refer to digital platform like digilocker , upi etc delivery ki service provide karwati hai

example :- adhar and digilocker

jesse pehle passport banna hota tha to document leke office ke chakkar lagana padta tha but recent scenario mein aap pasport ke liye apply kar dijiye or passport ka verification digilocker ke through ho jata hai or passport ghar aajtaa hai or upi ka more than 8B ka transication hota hai

pehle phyiscal infrastructre ko backbone mana jata tha lekin ab digital infrastructre ko backcbone mana jata hai

Digital Identity Systems ex of adhar , digital payment system like upi , data exchange frame work for the data sharing
